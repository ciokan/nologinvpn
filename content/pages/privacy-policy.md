## What We Collect

Nothing that identifies you.

- **Access key hash** — we store a hashed version of your access key. The original key is never stored.
- **Subscription expiry** — so we know whether your plan is active.
- **Device public keys** — WireGuard/Amnezia public keys you register. Private keys are generated in your browser and never transmitted.
- **Payment references** — transaction IDs for Stripe and crypto payments. We don't store full card details.

## What We Don't Collect

- No email address
- No name
- No IP address logs
- No connection timestamps
- No bandwidth usage
- No DNS query logs

## Third Parties

- **Stripe** — used for card payments. Stripe's privacy policy applies to data you enter on their checkout page.
- **Crypto payments** — fully on-chain. We only see a wallet address and a transaction reference.

## Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the dashboard.

## Changes

We'll update this page if anything changes. The date at the bottom reflects the last revision.
